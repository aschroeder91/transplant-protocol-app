# Pre-Op Transplant Checklist

## Verification
- [ ] Verify H&P
- [ ] Verify Surgical and Blood Consent
- [ ] Verify Anesthesia Consent
- [ ] Verify Type and Crossmatch
- [ ] Verify NPO Status
- [ ] Verify BMP
- [ ] Verify CBC
- [ ] Verify EKG
- [ ] Blood Glucose
- [ ] Endotool BS > 180
- [ ] Apply SCDs and TED hose
- [ ] Ensure functional Peripheral IV
- [ ] Betadine Nasal Swab
- [ ] Brush Teeth / CHG mouth wash
- [ ] Apply Warming Gown
- [ ] Obtain Pre-Op Vitals
- [ ] Weigh Patient

## Pre-Op Medications
- [ ] Ancef 2 g
- [ ] Mycophenolate 1000 mg PO
- [ ] Acetaminophen (Tylenol) tablet 1000 mg PO
- [ ] Pantoprazole 80 mg PO
- [ ] Chlorhexidine (Peridex) 0.12% solution 15 mL
- [ ] Lactated Ringers
- [ ] Continue beta blocker

## Notes
- Patients receiving a living donor will be admitted directly to Pre-Op.
- Patients receiving a kidney from a deceased donor will be admitted to the transplant unit on 10 south to check labs and dialysis as needed prior to transplantation.
- Labs, EKG, Type and Crossmatch should be completed by the floor.
- The OR RN will be responsible for checking the ABO compatibility and transplant paperwork prior to bringing the patient to the OR the day of surgery.
- Patients do not require a specialized isolation room in pre-op.
